### Key Features

- Custom jars with your own options
- Shake-to-pick with animations
- AI-powered suggestions (you don't have to use them)
- Gamification (streaks, achievements)
- Share jars via QR codes/links
- Offline support
- Multi-language support (English & Spanish)

### Target Audience

Overthinkers, indecisive people, anyone who's spent 20 minutes deciding where to eat. Skews millennial/Gen Z. **Note:** App is for anyone, not just millennial/Gen Z. You can use it for any purpose you want.

### Key Features

- Sponsor-sponsee pairing via invite codes
- Sobriety tracking with relapse workflows
- Journey timeline
- Task management
- 12-step content with steps, daily reflections, prayers, and meeting log
- Expense tracking (see how much you've saved)

### Program Tab (12-Step Support):

- Steps: All 12 steps with completion tracking that displays on your journey timeline
- Daily: Daily reflections sourced from AA.org
- Prayers: Step prayers and other common recovery prayers
- Meet: Meeting log to record attendance, displayed on your journey timeline

### Target Audience

People in recovery (AA, NA, any program), sponsors, sponsees. Adults 25-55 typically. **Note:** App is for anyone in sobriety/recovery, not just 12-step programs. You can turn off the 12-step content if you don't want it.
